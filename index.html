My story

I am Meenakshi Mehta

With over 24 years of experience across diverse landscapes from Wellness and Channel Marketing to Experiential Training - Meenakshi is more than a professional; she is a weaver of dreams and a master of detail.

Her life took a pivotal turn when she faced a profound battle with cancer. What could have been a story of loss became a testament to fierce resilience. Today, she blends her corporate expertise in MarCom consulting with her personal journey of recovery to inspire others. Whether she is crafting a marketing strategy or sharing her story on stage, Meenakshi’s heart beats for transformation and authentic storytelling.
